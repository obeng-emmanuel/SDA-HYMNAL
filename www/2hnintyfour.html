294 – Power in the Blood

1.

Would you be free
from the burden of sin?
There’s pow’r in the blood,
pow’r in the blood;
Would you o’er evil
a victory win?
There’s wonderful
power in the blood.

Refrain
There is pow’r, pow’r,
wonder working pow’r
In the blood
of the Lamb;
There is pow’r, pow’r,
wonder working pow’r
In the precious
blood of the Lamb.

2
Would be free from
your passion and pride?
There’s pow’r in the blood,
pow’r in the blood;
Come for a cleansing
to Calvary’s tide?
There’s wonderful
power in the blood.

3
Would you do service
for Jesus your King?
There’s pow’r in the blood,
pow’r in the blood;
Would you live daily
His praises to sing?
There’s wonderful
power in the blood.
